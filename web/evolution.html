Emergence of Secret Handshakes

Nothing here is designed. Each cell is an agent with a heritable recognition prefix and a trait for whether it is a mimic. Agents play the gated prisoner’s dilemma against their neighbours, copy whichever neighbour scored best, and mutate. Watch same-prefix cooperating clusters (one colour) form, mimics (dark dots) invade them, the post-recognition regime decide whether the mimics win or fizzle, and the recognition prefix turn over as cheats are escaped.
