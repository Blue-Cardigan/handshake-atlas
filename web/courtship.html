The Silent Stages

The recognition layer the handshake atlas leaves out: an observer infers a partner’s hidden type from noisy, graded reciprocations, escalating probe intensity and approaching only when the posterior that the partner is genuinely interested crosses a threshold. A mimic reciprocates cheap probes but balks at costly ones — so escalation, not a cleverer opener, is what exposes it.
